BODY MASS INDEX CALCULATOR (BMI)

BMI stands for Body Mass Index, which is a measurement used to determine if a person's weight is within a healthy range based on their height.

BMI index range Weight status
Less than 18.5 Underweight
18.5 to 24.9 Normal weight
25.0 to 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obese

Note:the World Health Organization (WHO) provides different BMI categories for men and women based on the same BMI values. According to the WHO, a BMI of 18.5-24.9 is considered a healthy weight range for both men and women, but a BMI of 25-29.9 is classified as overweight for men, while a BMI of 25-29.9 is classified as pre-obese for women. This reflects the fact that women tend to have a higher percentage of body fat than men, and may be at increased risk for certain health conditions at lower BMI levels.
